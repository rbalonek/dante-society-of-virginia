Tuesday, May 19, 2026 · 4:00 PM

Film: Valentino: The Last Emperor

Join the Dante Society for a screening of Valentino: The Last Emperor at Westminster Canterbury, 3rd Floor Commons. This look into the lavish times of the iconic designer is made with humor and an eye for detail, documenting the dramatic closing of the last true couturier's career and work. The film highlights the planning of Valentino's 45th anniversary show in Rome and the end of old-school handmade couture in the modern business of fashion.

Dinner: Following the film at Isabella's, 6:00 PM. Reservations: Pat Bradbury, (910) 695-5644 or [email protected].

Parking & Directions: Westminster Canterbury from V.E.S. Road. Park in visitor parking lots (immediate right or left). Walk towards buildings, enter the Main Entrance (building on your left). Turn right, take elevator to 3rd floor. Turn right — the Commons is the room straight ahead.
