Email Reminder Format

Program reminders are sent by email approximately one week before each event. Each reminder will include:

  • Event: Name and type of program
  • Date & Time: Day and start time
  • Location: Venue address and parking instructions
  • Description: Program details and speaker/performer information
  • Dinner: Post-event dinner details (if applicable) with reservation contact
  • RSVP: How to confirm attendance

This standardized format ensures members always have the key information at a glance.
