Become a Member

Funding for the Virginia Society is provided by its membership, which is open to all who have a love of all things Italian. Your membership makes our programs possible — from lectures and musical performances to Carnevale celebrations and film series.

The Dante Society's fiscal year runs September through September. Annual dues are payable in September and are requested by our signature Viva l'Italia event on September 20th.

Member Benefits

  • Invitations to all monthly programs, lectures, and events
  • Film & dinner series participation
  • Carnevale celebration access
  • Advance notice of special events and collaborations with Opera on the James, the Maier Museum, and other cultural organizations
  • Travel opportunities with fellow members
  • Monthly email updates and program reminders
  • Connection to a vibrant community of Italian-culture enthusiasts

How to Pay Dues

We do not accept Zelle. Please use one of the following methods:

Option 1: Mail a Check
Make checks payable to Dante Society of Virginia and mail to:
P.O. Box 131
Forest, VA 24551
Option 2: Bank Bill Pay
Use your bank's online bill pay feature to send a payment to:
Dante Society of Virginia
P.O. Box 131, Forest, VA 24551

Online Payment (Coming Soon)

We are working on offering an online payment option. For now, please use one of the methods above. No payment system is connected on this site — all dues are processed offline via check or bank bill pay.
